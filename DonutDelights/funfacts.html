Some Truth that "Cops love Doughnuts"

In the 50's, police officers on the gravyard shift
would stop at doughnut shops,
which were among the fewest establishments open late.
Eventually a reciprocal relationship developed.
Doughnut shop owners welcomed the protection,
and officers liked having
a place to chow down late at night.
So the association stuck around.
