About Us

The journey of Donut Delights began in 1971 by Melvin Furballski. Furballski is an immigrant baker coming form the far east coast of Poland. He was known for his delicate pasteries and fled Poland to find a better life for his family. Furballski landed in New York City in 1970 and got on a bus heading west. The bus experienced mechanical failure which forced a three day delay in Champaign, Illinois. While there Furballksi felt the need for a donut shop so he decided to stay and start a new life in Urbana, Illinois. With just one hundred dollars and finding an abandoned breadshop, Donut Delights was up and running. Within two days the donuts were flying off the shelves and he couldn't bake fast enough. Since the opening things have never slowed down. Now today the classic recipe donut is till around along with dozens more made with the same love and care since it opened.

In the mid-1980's Donut Delights was destroyed by a fire. Losing the shop's records but the recipes were safe. Many people thought that the fire was the end of the shop but Furballksi proved them wrong and used it to reconfigure the interior which let him add on a coffee roastery section. Now when customers enter, the aroma of freshly roasted coffee beans and freshly baked donuts fills the shop. It's the closest thing to "Breakfast Heaven" anywhere in the C-U area!
