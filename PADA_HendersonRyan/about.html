A bit about us

We first opened on July 4th, 2003 in the heart of Champaign. Our initial goal was to serve people in the Champaign area, helping to fix software issues in their computers. We continued like this for 3 years, until 2006 when we expanded our shop to encompass hardware problems as well. Through this expansion, we were able to improve our staff's knowledge, as well as diagnose and solve a wider range of problems. We were also the first support business in the area to assist with smartphone technology, expanding our company even further. We hope to in the future expand more, continuing to lead the industry of device troubleshooting and repair.
