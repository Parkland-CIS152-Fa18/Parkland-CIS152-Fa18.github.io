Lauren and Keegan were in the same college course when the topic walruses was brought up.
Together, they had the idea to create one of the leading organizations to save walruses. WeRWalrus is a non-profit
organization that helps rescue and treat walruses in danger. We provide hospitality and care towards fellow walruses. We team up with sponsors around the world and together we save. Join the movement and save them!
