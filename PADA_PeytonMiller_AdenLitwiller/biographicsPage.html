Classic Rock Legends and Their Information

Rolling Stones - Eric Clapton: Rollingstones.com

Born: March 30, 1945

Eric Clapton is the only three-time inductee to the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. He also ranked second in the Rolling Stones list of 100 greatest guitarists of all time.

Freddie Mercury Website: Freddiemercury.com

Born: September 5, 1946

Died: November 24, 1991

Freddie Mercury was the lead vocalist of Queen. Recognized as one of the greatest singers in music history. He had a four-octave vocal range.

Billy Joel Website: Billyjoel.com

Born: May 9, 1949

Billy Joel is a composer and pianist. Nickname is the Piano Man. He has been making music since the 1960's.

Ringo Starr Website: Ringostarr.com

Born: July 7, 1940

Ringo Starr is an English musician and actor. He gained his fame as the drummer for the Beatles. His full actual name is Richard Starkey.

Jon Bon Jovi Website: Bonjovi.imdb.com

Born: March 2, 1962

Jon Bon Jovi is an American singer/songwriter. He is the founder of Bon Jovi, it was founded in 1983.
