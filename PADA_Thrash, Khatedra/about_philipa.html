about us: philipa

Philipa began her career in event planning in 2001 working first at various boutique firms, and finally with designer Serfio Yanel and his eponymous design firm in Chicago, Illinois. After 10 years with one of the biggest event planners in the country, Philipa established S-Design in her native New York City with the intent of bringing her skills and experience to a clientele for whom her services were previously out of reach. Priding herself on realizing her clients’ dreams, Philipa built a reputation for her company that matched her slogan “If you can dream it, I can design it, no matter the budget.” At P&T Landscape and Design Philipa continues her work in partnership with the florists at Flowers on Fifty-Third
