about us: tedra

Tedra inherited the family business, Flowers on Fifty-Third in 2008, and has been working in the shop all her life. The family business has expanded greatly over the years and under her stewardship the shop continues to serve the midtown area with the best flowers in the city. Having a special emphasis on flower arranging, Tedra personally oversees the creation of the shop’s seasonal arrangements, and the desgins offered for home and event decoration. In partnership with Philipa Selwin since 2015, P&T Landscape and Design has opened yet another chapter in the shop’s history, and it’s shaping up to be the best yet!
