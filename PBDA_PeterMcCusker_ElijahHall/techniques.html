Running Tips

The importance of correct running form cannot be overstressed. Running the "right way" greatly minimizes the chances of injury while maximizing your speed and comfort. By developing good habits early on, you are setting yourself up for a long and successful running career. Go over this checklist in your head while you run and feel the improvement!

Form

Upper Body

  • Run with your chest up: Allowing the maximium amount of oxygen to flow into your lungs.
  • Utilize a slight lean of approximately 5°: This generates forward momentum and helps with propulsion.
  • With elbows bent at a 90° angle swing arms: Keep in mind to drive your elbows back on the back swing and up on the forward swing.

Lower Body

  • Pick up the knees
  • Open up the stride

Breathing Tips

Knowing the simple tools to controlling your breath can create tons of benefit for your body. The general rules are as follows:

  • Breathe in through the nose and out through the mouth.
  • Establish and familiarize yourself with a breathing rhythm. (This could follow the rhythm of your strides)

Mindset

By making use of the afore mentioned tips concerning form and breathing, you can mentally rest assured that you are maximizing the effectiveness of the run. On top of that, consider these following tips to further increase efficiency.

  • Develop a word or phrase that you can repeat in your head to take your mind off of the run.
  • Run with music: This can even pair with your breathing rhythm.
