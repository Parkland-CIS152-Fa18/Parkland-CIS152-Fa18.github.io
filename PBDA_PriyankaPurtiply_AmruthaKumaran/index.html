Twin City Photography was founded by two friends studying in the photography department at UIUC in 2001. We wanted to create a wonderful service that offered a variety of high quality prints and other media to the public at reasonable prices. Most of all, we wanted to create a real connection with our customers and make photoshoots fun, relaxed, and sucessful in capturing their most cherished memories. 17 years on we believe we are fulfilling this mission everyday with our work and have been changing the way people think about photoshoots all over Champaign-Urbana.


What's great about Twin City Photography is that we offer reasonable prices and a wide variety of options for how you would like to receive your final product. We provide all types of digital files for you to use or print with the help of a third party. We also have a clear and competitive price listing if you choose to use our printing service if you want to purchase prints from us directly.


We are willing to make any media product to your specifications, including a personalized vine if that's what you want!
